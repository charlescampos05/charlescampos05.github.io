Project Overview

Tourism is one of the sectors most hit by the covid-19 during this pandemic. Understanding the links between pandemics and travel is critical to understanding global health security and change.
Tourist behavior and mental health, as well as businesses and others, are negatively impacted by the pandemic.
As a result, this year's pandemic, also known as Covid-19, has put our lives in danger of being infected with a deadly virus, and many tourist attractions have closed their doors to guests as a result of the epidemic.

And now, vaccine is available and most of us were already administered the vaccine and boarders from different provinces are now opening their gates from travelers.
With this website, we will encourage travelers by posting photos, video clips and important information's here in ELYU. This website also aims to guide travelers on the documents needed in entering the boarder of ELYU.

In conclusion

We are not only bringing back the glory days of travel and tours on our province, but also we are bringing back the livelihood of our citizens and the economy of our province

Key Problems

Many tourist cannot travel because of travel restriction to our area

Need a private vehicle for transportation and insufficient public transportation

Not vaccinated or refused to administer the vaccine.

Solutions

Vaccine - get vaccinated

Overview of how ELYU handle COVID-19 –parameters of preventions and safety precautions of the province and the good result
